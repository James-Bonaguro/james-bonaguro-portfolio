The Challenge

CCC Intelligent Solutions operated with complete organizational blindness to customer satisfaction and internal performance across their entire software portfolio. Leadership had no understanding of what customers actually felt after implementations, deliveries, software updates, or training sessions.

The organization suffered from multiple critical blind spots: inconsistent survey questions meant no reliable NPS data, some customers never received surveys due to manual process failures, and responses that were collected disappeared into a black hole with no follow-up or action.

There was zero accountability for customer outcomes - product managers, trainers, and implementation teams had no feedback loop to understand their performance or drive improvement. The company was making strategic decisions based on assumptions rather than customer intelligence.

The Solution

Designed and led implementation of enterprise customer intelligence automation system that transformed CCC's relationship with their automotive ecosystem.

Collaborated directly with Salesforce development teams to architect real-time survey distribution system with automated workflows, standardized data collection, and interactive executive dashboards.

Conducted comprehensive UATD (User Acceptance Testing and Documentation) to ensure system reliability and user adoption across diverse stakeholder groups.

Business Impact

  • Immediate customer recovery system: Customers providing negative feedback received immediate phone calls for issue resolution, transforming potential churn into customer loyalty
  • Transformed organizational decision-making from assumptions to data-driven customer intelligence
  • Established systematic accountability loop ensuring every customer interaction drives measurable outcomes
  • Created standardized customer intelligence framework enabling reliable NPS tracking and performance comparison
  • Delivered weekly strategic insights to sales, product, and executive leadership teams for immediate action
  • Implemented automated Salesforce triggers ensuring every piece of customer feedback generates appropriate follow-up

Process Efficiency Gains: 90% reduction in manual work, 115% improvement in customer satisfaction scores, elimination of inconsistent data collection across customer base.

Technical Implementation

Salesforce Integration
Built custom workflow triggers and automation rules that eliminated manual survey distribution while maintaining data quality and compliance standards.
Real-Time Automation
Implemented immediate survey distribution upon implementation completion, with automated reminder sequences and escalation protocols.
Interactive Dashboards
Developed live-updating executive dashboards providing both product and sales teams with actionable customer insights for immediate response.
Standardization at Scale
Created unified survey methodology across diverse customer segments while maintaining relevance for repair shops, dealerships, insurance carriers, and OEMs.

Strategic Value for Enterprise Software Companies

This transformation demonstrates the critical intersection of technical implementation expertise and customer success strategy that enterprise software companies require.

The project showcased ability to collaborate with development teams, manage complex stakeholder requirements, and deliver measurable business outcomes that directly impact revenue and customer retention.

Most importantly, it created a scalable framework for understanding customer behavior and driving product decisions based on real-time feedback rather than assumptions.

The Challenge

CCC's go-to-market operations were chaotic and fragmented across multiple product lines. Teams relied on disorganized email chains, scattered SharePoint repositories, and inconsistent project management approaches.

While the dysfunction was frustrating, it created a culture of unaccountability where team members could deflect responsibility with excuses like "I didn't know it was in SharePoint," "no one told me," or "it got lost in the emails."

The organization needed unified project management infrastructure, but the greater challenge was getting buy-in from people who preferred the chaos because it allowed them to avoid clear ownership and accountability.

Implementation Strategy

Led comprehensive Asana enterprise rollout across 150+ stakeholders spanning entire organization.

Developed standardized workflows, templates, and project structures where all go-to-market activities were housed in one single location with full visibility and accountability for every team member and project.

Conducted extensive training programs and built comprehensive support infrastructure to ensure adoption across diverse team functions, with ongoing optimization based on user feedback and organizational growth.

Implementation Phases

Discovery & Planning
Analyzed existing workflows, identified pain points, and recruited champions across all go-to-market functions to build internal advocacy network and drive organizational buy-in.
Template Development
Created standardized project templates with daily and weekly optimization, developed new onboarding processes, license management systems, and created organizational hype video to drive excitement and adoption.
Support Infrastructure
Established dedicated support portal, email helpdesk, resource team within Asana, and continuous user feedback surveys to ensure sustained adoption and ongoing optimization.
Enterprise Deployment
Executed phased rollout with comprehensive training, change management campaign, and support systems that enabled rapid organic expansion beyond original go-to-market scope.

Measurable Impact

  • Achieved >25% increase in GTM organization productivity through improved project visibility and coordination
  • Accelerated product launches and feature releases by up to 4 weeks across all product lines
  • Transformed accountability culture: Eliminated excuse-making through transparent task ownership where every person's name was attached to their work
  • Enhanced go-to-market efficiency through standardized workflows and automated progress tracking
  • Drove organic expansion beyond original scope - tracking KPIs and migrating entire marketing department from a competing tool to Asana
  • Created scalable support infrastructure enabling rapid organizational adoption and continuous optimization

Key Success Factors

Change Management Campaign: Overcame resistance through champion recruitment, organizational hype video, and comprehensive support infrastructure rather than just technical implementation.

Accountability Through Transparency: Transformed organizational culture by making work ownership visible, eliminating the ability to deflect responsibility through system dysfunction.

Continuous Support System: Established ongoing training programs, user surveys, dedicated help portal, and resource teams to ensure sustained adoption and optimization.

Realistic Expectations: Acknowledged implementation challenges while building comprehensive support to overcome resistance and drive meaningful organizational change.
